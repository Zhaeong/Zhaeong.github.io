The Devil and Daniel Johnston

Sat July 4th, 2015

True love will find you in the end. I’ve been recently getting into the music of Daniel Johnston, there’s just something completely appealing about outsider music, someone with little to no instrument proficiency, using only his/her heart, I hope that’s not too sentimental. The movie was similar to Crumb, another documentary about abnormal geniuses. I’m not sure if they tried to make you feel sorry for him, due to his illness. He’s got such an innocent voice though. Angelic. Trying to remember, but my feelings can’t know for sure. Try to reach out, but it’s gone.
